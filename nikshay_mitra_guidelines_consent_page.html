Nikshay Mitra — Guidelines

Key Guidelines

  • Support should align with Nikshay Mitra categories (nutrition kits, diagnostics, livelihood assistance) and local program direction.
  • Respect patient privacy. Share or use patient information only for authorized coordination.
  • Maintain consistency—prefer monthly/quarterly commitments you can reliably fulfill.
  • Coordinate deliveries with the program team/health facility; do not contact patients directly unless arranged.
